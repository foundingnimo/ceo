# Operational Excellence & Execution: The CEO's Execution Engine

Strategy without execution is just a wish list. As CEO, your job isn't to manage every process—it's to build an **organization that turns strategy into results** with speed, consistency, and continuous improvement.

## Course Overview
This course transforms you from a "hope it works out" CEO to a systems-thinking operator who scales through process, data, and empowerment—not micromanagement.

### What You Will Learn:
1. **Process Optimization**: Lean thinking and Six Sigma—eliminating waste, not adding bureaucracy.
2. **Data-Driven Decisions**: Building the KPI dashboards that give you operational visibility.
3. **Psychological Safety & Empowerment**: Scaling through trust, not command-and-control.
4. **Strategic Alignment**: Ensuring operations serve the vision, not the other way around.
5. **Technology Enablement**: Automation and digital transformation without the hype.

## Your Instructors (via curated content):
- **Taiichi Ohno**: Father of the Toyota Production System (Lean).
- **W. Edwards Deming**: The quality guru who rebuilt Japanese manufacturing.
- **Amy Edmondson**: Harvard professor and pioneer of Psychological Safety.

## How to use this course:
- **Read/Watch**: Each episode mixes punchy text with curated videos and articles.
- **Do the Action Items**: These are 10-20 minute exercises you can do *today*. Skip these, and you've just consumed entertainment.
- **Apply Daily**: Operations is a practice, not a class. Use these frameworks in your next 1:1, standup, or board meeting.

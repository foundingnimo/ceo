# Strategic Planning & Vision: A Masterclass for CEOs

Welcome to your strategic command center. This course is designed specifically for CEOs who need to move from tactical firefighting to long-term vision and execution.

## Course Overview
In this course, we will strip away the jargon and focus on the high-leverage frameworks that define successful technology companies. Each episode is designed to be consumed in **5 to 45 minutes**, fitting into your busy schedule.

### What You Will Learn:
1. **The North Star**: How to define why your company exists.
2. **The Landscape**: How to analyze your competitors and your environment.
3. **The Edge**: How to find and protect your unique competitive advantage.
4. **The Execution**: how to translate high-level strategy into daily wins using OKRs.

## How to use this course:
- **Watch/Read**: Each episode contains a mix of text and curated videos.
- **Do the Exercises**: Each lesson ends with a "CEO Action Item". Don't skip these—they are where the real learning happens.
- **Iterate**: Strategic planning is a muscle. The more you use these frameworks, the stronger your vision becomes.

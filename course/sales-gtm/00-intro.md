# Sales & Go-to-Market: The CEO's Revenue Playbook

Even the best product fails without effective go-to-market execution. As CEO, you are the chief revenue architect—responsible for building the engine that turns your vision into paying customers.

## Course Overview
This course strips away the sales jargon and focuses on the strategic decisions that separate companies with predictable revenue from those stuck in founder-led chaos. Each episode is designed to be consumed in **5 to 45 minutes**.

### What You Will Learn:
1. **The Target**: How to define your Ideal Customer Profile with surgical precision.
2. **The Message**: How to articulate value that resonates with different buyers.
3. **The Motion**: How to choose between PLG, Sales-Led, and Hybrid approaches.
4. **The Machine**: How to build repeatable, scalable sales processes.
5. **The Metrics**: How to measure what matters and optimize relentlessly.
6. **The Alignment**: How to make Sales, Marketing, and Product work as one engine.

## How to Use This Course:
- **Watch/Read**: Each episode contains a mix of text and curated videos.
- **Do the Exercises**: Each lesson ends with a "CEO Action Item". These 10-20 minute exercises are where strategy becomes reality.
- **Iterate**: GTM is never "done." The best companies continuously refine their approach.

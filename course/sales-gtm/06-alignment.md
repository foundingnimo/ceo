## Part 3: The Marketing â†” Sales SLA

Create a written agreement between Marketing and Sales:

**Marketing commits to:**
- Deliver X MQLs per month that meet these criteria: [list]
- Average lead score of Y
- Response to Sales feedback within 48 hours

**Sales commits to:**
- Follow up on every MQL within 4 hours
- Update CRM with disposition (qualified/disqualified + reason)
- Provide win/loss feedback to Marketing monthly

Without an SLA, blame replaces accountability.

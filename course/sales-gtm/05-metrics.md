## Part 3: Sales Velocity

How fast are you making money? Sales velocity combines four levers:

**Sales Velocity = (# Opportunities Ã— Avg Deal Size Ã— Win Rate) / Sales Cycle Length**

| Lever | How to Improve It |
|:------|:------------------|
| **# Opportunities** | More pipeline (marketing, SDRs, expansion) |
| **Deal Size** | Upsell, bundle, target larger ICP |
| **Win Rate** | Better qualification, sales training, positioning |
| **Sales Cycle** | Remove friction, multi-thread deals |

When velocity stalls, diagnose *which lever* is broken before throwing more reps at the problem.

## Part 5: Leading vs. Lagging Indicators

| Leading (Predictive) | Lagging (Outcome) |
|:---------------------|:------------------|
| Pipeline created this month | Revenue closed this month |
| Discovery calls completed | Win rate |
| Demo requests | Average deal size |
| Website traffic | Churn rate |

**CEO insight:** Your weekly sales meeting should review *leading* indicators. By the time lagging metrics move, it's too late to course-correct.

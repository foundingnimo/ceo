# Episode 7: Mastering the Cash Flow Statement

**Estimated Time:** 40 Minutes

The Cash Flow Statement is often called the most important financial statement because **cash is king**. It explains exactly where cash came from and where it went.

## Part 1: Why Cash Flow Matters More Than Profit

**You can't pay employees with profit. You can't pay rent with profit. You pay with CASH.**

The cash flow statement reconciles the difference between:
- What the income statement says (profit/loss)
- What actually happened in your bank account (cash change)

## Part 2: Three Sections of Cash Flow

| Section | What It Shows | Healthy Sign |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| **Operating Activities** | Cash from running the business | Positive (at scale) |
| **Investing Activities** | Cash from buying/selling assets | Negative (you're investing) |
| **Financing Activities** | Cash from investors/lenders | Positive (if growing) |

**The Complete Picture:**
```
Cash from Operating Activities     +/- 
Cash from Investing Activities     +/-
Cash from Financing Activities     +/-
= Net Change in Cash               +/-

Beginning Cash + Net Change = Ending Cash
```

## Part 3: Operating Cash Flow Deep Dive

Starts with Net Income, then adjusts:

| Adjustment | Why? |
|------------|------|
| + Depreciation | Expense but no cash out |
| - Increase in AR | Revenue recognized but cash not collected |
| + Increase in AP | Expenses incurred but not paid yet |
| - Increase in Inventory | Cash spent on inventory |

## Part 4: Interpreting Cash Flow Patterns

| Company Stage | Operating | Investing | Financing |
|---------------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Healthy Growing** | Negative | Negative | Positive |
| **Mature Profitable** | Positive | Neg/Neutral | Negative |
| **Struggling** | Negative | Positive | Positive |

## Part 5: Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Meaning |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Free Cash Flow** | Operating CF - CapEx | Cash after maintaining business |
| **Burn Rate** | Monthly cash consumption | How fast you're spending |
| **Runway** | Cash / Monthly Burn | Months until out of cash |

## CEO Action Item: Cash Flow Analysis

1. What's your monthly net burn rate?
2. What's your current runway?
3. Is operating cash flow trending toward positive?

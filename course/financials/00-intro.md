# Financial Literacy: The CEO's Guide

**Financial literacy is the cornerstone of effective CEO decision-making.** While no formal certification is required, a deep understanding of financial principles enables you to make informed strategic choices, attract investors, manage cash flow, and ensure your company's long-term sustainability.

Many tech startups fail due to cash flow mismanagement, making this competency critical for survival and growth.

## Course Overview

This course takes you from zero financial knowledge to CEO-level financial fluency. You'll learn to speak the language of finance, read financial statements with confidence, and apply financial frameworks to strategic decisions.

### What You Will Learn:
1. **Financial Foundations**: The language, concepts, and statements of finance.
2. **Cash Flow Mastery**: The #1 skill for startup survival.
3. **Budgeting & Forecasting**: Translating strategy into numbers.
4. **Unit Economics**: Proving your business model works.
5. **Strategic Finance**: Making data-driven executive decisions.

## Course Structure

| Module | Episodes | Focus |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Foundation | 1-4 | Financial vocabulary and core concepts |
| Statements | 5-7 | Mastering the three financial statements |
| Cash & Planning | 8-10 | Cash flow, budgeting, forecasting |
| Metrics | 11-13 | Unit economics, pricing, KPIs |
| Strategy | 14-15 | Investor relations, strategic decisions |

**Total Time:** 12-15 hours across 15 episodes (5-45 minutes each)

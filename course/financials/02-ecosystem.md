## Part 2: Who Cares About Your Finances?

| Stakeholder | What They Care About | Why |
|-------------|---------------------|-----|
| **Investors** | Growth and return | Their money is at stake |
| **Board** | Transparency and strategy | Governance responsibility |
| **Employees** | Stability | Job security depends on it |
| **Customers** | Your longevity | They rely on your product |
| **Banks/Lenders** | Creditworthiness | Repayment ability |

## Part 3: The Financial Calendar

| Cadence | What To Review |
|---------|----------------|
| **Weekly** | Cash position, collections, urgent issues |
| **Monthly** | Financial statements, budget variance, runway |
| **Quarterly** | Board reporting, forecast updates, KPI trends |
| **Annually** | Strategic budget, financial planning, audits |

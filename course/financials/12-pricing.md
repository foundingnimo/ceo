## Part 5: Testing and Optimizing

**How to test pricing:**
1. Test with new customers (don't confuse existing)
2. Track conversion rates at different price points
3. Measure impact on LTV, not just initial sale
4. Consider A/B testing different price points

**Grandfather clauses:**
- Protect existing customers from increases
- Apply new prices to new customers only
- Gradually migrate or maintain indefinitely

## Part 1: Types of Profit Margins

| Margin | Formula | What It Shows |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Gross Margin** | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | Profit after direct costs |
| **Contribution Margin** | (Revenue - Variable Costs) / Revenue | Per-unit profitability |
| **Operating Margin** | Operating Income / Revenue | Operational efficiency |
| **Net Margin** | Net Income / Revenue | Bottom-line profitability |

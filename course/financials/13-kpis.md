## Part 3: The Rule of 40

```
Rule of 40 = Revenue Growth Rate + Profit Margin
```

| Example | Growth | Margin | Score |
|---------|--------|--------|-------|
| Growth-focused | 60% | -20% | 40% ‚úì |
| Balanced | 35% | 10% | 45% ‚úì |
| Profit-focused | 15% | 30% | 45% ‚úì |
| Underperforming | 20% | 10% | 30% ‚ùå |

## Part 4: Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

The most important SaaS metric:

```
NRR = (Start MRR + Expansion - Churn - Contraction) / Start MRR
```

| NRR | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| **< 90%** | Bucket is leaking badly |
| **90-100%** | Treading water |
| **100-110%** | Good, expansion covers churn |
| **110-130%** | Excellent |
| **> 130%** | World-class |

## Part 5: Building Your CEO Dashboard

**Weekly:**
- Cash position
- New deals closed  
- Pipeline value
- Failed payments

**Monthly:**
- MRR/ARR and customer count
- Churn rate
- Burn rate and runway
- Budget vs. actual

**Quarterly:**
- LTV:CAC by segment
- Net revenue retention
- Rule of 40 score
- Unit economics trends

# Board Management & Investor Relations: A Masterclass for CEOs

Welcome to your stakeholder command center. This course is designed for CEOs who need to build trust with their board, communicate effectively with investors, and leverage these relationships as strategic assets.

## Why This Matters

For startup and scale-up CEOs, your board and investors aren't just capital sources—they're critical partners who can provide guidance, connections, and support during the inevitable challenges of building a company. Mismanage these relationships, and you risk losing confidence, resources, or even your job.

The good news: these skills can be learned. This course takes you from the fundamentals to practical mastery.

## Course Overview

Each episode is designed to be consumed in **5 to 45 minutes**, fitting into your busy schedule.

### What You Will Learn:

| Episode | Topic | Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Board Fundamentals** — Roles, responsibilities, and dynamics | 25 min |
| 2 | **Transparency & Trust** — The foundation of every relationship | 20 min |
| 3 | **Board Meetings** — Running meetings that matter | 30 min |
| 4 | **Investor Communication** — Updates, expectations, and difficult conversations | 25 min |
| 5 | **Leveraging Your Board** — Getting real value from your directors | 20 min |
| 6 | **Crisis Communication** — When things go wrong | 25 min |

## How to Use This Course

- **Watch/Read**: Each episode contains curated videos, articles, and clear explanations.
- **Do the Exercises**: Each lesson ends with a "CEO Action Item"—a 10-20 minute exercise you can complete today.
- **Apply Immediately**: Unlike strategy or product, board management skills are immediately testable. Use your next board meeting as a practice ground.

# Technical Competence & Digital Literacy: A Masterclass for CEOs

Welcome to your technology command center. This course is designed for CEOs who need to make informed technology decisions, communicate effectively with engineering teams, and understand technical trade-offs—without writing a single line of code.

## Why This Matters

In a tech company, technical illiteracy at the CEO level is a strategic vulnerability. You don't need to code, but you do need to:
- **Ask the right questions** about technology investments
- **Understand trade-offs** when your CTO presents options
- **Communicate effectively** with engineering teams
- **Recognize risks** like technical debt and security vulnerabilities

This course gives you the vocabulary, frameworks, and confidence to lead technology decisions.

## Course Overview

Each episode is designed to be consumed in **5 to 45 minutes**, fitting into your busy schedule.

## How to Use This Course

- **Watch/Read**: Each episode contains curated videos, articles, and clear explanations.
- **Don't memorize**: Focus on understanding concepts well enough to ask good questions.
- **Do the Exercises**: Each lesson ends with a "CEO Action Item"—apply what you learn immediately.
- **Engage your CTO**: Use these lessons as conversation starters with your technical leaders.

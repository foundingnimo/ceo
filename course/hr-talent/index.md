# Human Resources & Talent Management for CEOs

Welcome to the HR & Talent Management curriculum. This course is designed to take you from "zero" to a strategic leader who understands that in a tech company, **talent is your primary competitive advantage.**

## Curriculum Overview

This course is structured into 8 bite-sized episodes (5-45 minutes each). You can follow them in order or jump to the areas where you need the most immediate help.

| Episode | Title | Duration | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01** | [Why HR Matters](01-why-hr-matters.md) | 15 mins | Strategic HR vs. Administrative HR |
| **02** | [Defining Talent Needs](02-defining-talent-needs.md) | 20 mins | Skills-based hiring & ICP for Talent |
| **03** | [Employer Branding](03-employer-branding.md) | 15 mins | Attracting talent through authenticity |
| **04** | [Recruiting: Art & Science](04-recruiting-art-science.md) | 30 mins | Interviews & the CEO's role in hiring |
| **05** | [Onboarding Excellence](05-onboarding-excellence.md) | 25 mins | Integrating new hires for long-term success |
| **06** | [High Performance Culture](06-performance-culture.md) | 45 mins | Feedback, OKRs, and the "Keeper Test" |
| **07** | [Compensation Strategy](07-compensation-strategy.md) | 35 mins | Equity, Stock Options, and Motivation |
| **08** | [Scaling & HR Ops](08-scaling-and-hr-ops.md) | 20 mins | When and how to scale the People function |

---

## How to use this course
- **Read the theory**: Each lesson starts with a brief conceptual overview.
- **Watch/Read external resources**: Deepen your knowledge with curated high-quality content.
- **Take Action**: Every lesson ends with a "CEO Action Item" to apply the learning to your company.

> [!TIP]
> Start with Episode 01 to understand how to leverage your HR lead (or future HR lead) as a strategic business partner.
